How to get your token (click to expand)
  1. Open https://makerworld.com in your browser and sign in normally.
  2. Open DevTools — press F12 (or right-click → Inspect).
  3. Go to the Application tab (Chrome/Edge) or Storage tab (Firefox).
  4. In the left sidebar: Cookies → https://makerworld.com.
  5. Find the cookie named token. Click on it and copy the entire Value field (it's a long string starting with eyJ…).
  6. Paste below and click Save.

The token is your live MakerWorld session — keep it private. It's stored locally in data/accessToken.json and used only to authenticate calls to api.bambulab.com from this server.
